## Core
British English. NEVER use dashes as sentence punctuation - no em dash (—), no en dash (–), no spaced hyphen ( - ). The character does not matter, the usage does. Rewrite with commas, semicolons, colons, parentheses, or split into separate sentences. Hyphens are only allowed inside compound words (e.g. "half-serious", "event-sourcing", "first-company"). Applies to all prose, headings, meta tags, and placeholder content.

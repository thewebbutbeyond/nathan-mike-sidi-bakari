# 05: Curious Observer's Notes Reading

**Project:** Nathan Mike Sidi Bakari
**Created:** 2026-04-21
**Method:** Whiteport Design Studio (WDS)

---

## Transaction

**What this scenario covers:**
A visitor opens Notes, reads a curated long-form piece, and optionally follows through RSS.

---

## Business Goal

**Goal:** Enable open exploration and optional following.
**Objective:** Notes function as curated editorial writing rather than raw logs.

---

## User & Situation

**Visitor Mode:** Curious Observer (Secondary)
**Situation:** They are less interested in a specific project and more interested in Nathan's thinking.

---

## Mental State

**Trigger:** They notice Notes from the Home / Portal or an artifact.

**Hope:** They can read something clear, durable, and reflective.

**Worry:** Notes might be noisy, raw, or high-frequency logs with little editorial care.

---

## Device & Starting Point

**Device:** Mobile or desktop.
**Entry:** They open Notes from Home / Portal or from a related artifact.

---

## Best Outcome

**User Success:**
The visitor reads a complete note and understands that following via RSS is available but optional.

**Archive Success:**
Notes establish editorial depth without becoming the whole site.

---

## Shortest Path

1. **Notes Index** - Visitor scans curated notes with dates and summaries.
2. **Note Detail** - Visitor reads one long-form reflection in a comfortable layout.
3. **RSS** - Visitor sees the optional feed link and understands how to follow. ✓

---

## Trigger Map Connections

**Visitor Mode:** Curious Observer (Secondary)

**Driving Forces Addressed:**

- ✅ **Want:** Authentic context.
- ✅ **Want:** Non-linear discovery.
- ❌ **Fear:** Vague personal branding.
- ❌ **Fear:** Being pitched to.

**Archive Goal:** Enable open exploration.

---

## Scenario Steps

| Step | Folder | Purpose | Exit Action |
|------|--------|---------|-------------|
| 05.1 | `05.1-notes-index/` | Scan editorial notes by date and summary. | Open note. |
| 05.2 | `05.2-note-detail/` | Read a durable long-form reflection. | Notice RSS. |
| 05.3 | `05.3-rss/` | Optionally follow future notes. | Scenario success. |

---

## Phase 4 Notes

Notes should feel editorial and restrained. Avoid blog-feed noise and avoid turning RSS into a growth mechanic.

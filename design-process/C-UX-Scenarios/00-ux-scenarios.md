Scenarios organize the portfolio into meaningful journeys. For this project, the expected structure is non-linear rather than funnel-based.

Initial page candidates from discovery:

- Home / Portal
- Collections
- Timeline
- Selected
- Notes / Thoughts
- About
- Contact

Every artifact should be accessible through:

- **Identity:** collections and tags
- **Time:** chronological timeline

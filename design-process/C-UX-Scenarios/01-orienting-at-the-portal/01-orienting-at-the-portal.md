# 01: Curious Observer's Portal Orientation

**Project:** Nathan Mike Sidi Bakari
**Created:** 2026-04-21
**Method:** Whiteport Design Studio (WDS)

---

## Transaction

**What this scenario covers:**
A first-time visitor lands on the site, understands it as an archive, and chooses a selected artifact as an entry point.

---

## Business Goal

**Goal:** Enable non-linear exploration.
**Objective:** A new visitor can understand the archive model and choose a route within the first screen.

---

## User & Situation

**Visitor Mode:** Curious Observer (Secondary)
**Situation:** They arrive from a shared link or search result with mild curiosity and no specific task.

---

## Mental State

**Trigger:** They encounter Nathan's site and want to know what kind of place it is.

**Hope:** They can quickly find a meaningful first path without reading a pitch.

**Worry:** The site might be vague personal branding with no clear entry point.

---

## Device & Starting Point

**Device:** Desktop or mobile.
**Entry:** They land on the Home / Portal from a link, search result, or profile reference.

---

## Best Outcome

**User Success:**
The visitor understands the archive routes and opens one selected artifact that gives them real signal.

**Archive Success:**
The site demonstrates its structure without relying on a conversion CTA or resume-style narrative.

---

## Shortest Path

1. **Home / Portal** - Visitor sees the archive premise and routes into Selected, Timeline, Collections, and Notes.
2. **Selected** - Visitor scans a small curated subset of representative artifacts.
3. **Artifact Detail** - Visitor opens one artifact and sees context, metadata, and related paths. ✓

---

## Trigger Map Connections

**Visitor Mode:** Curious Observer (Secondary)

**Driving Forces Addressed:**

- ✅ **Want:** Quick orientation.
- ✅ **Want:** Non-linear discovery.
- ❌ **Fear:** No entry point.
- ❌ **Fear:** Vague personal branding.

**Archive Goal:** Enable non-linear exploration.

---

## Scenario Steps

| Step | Folder | Purpose | Exit Action |
|------|--------|---------|-------------|
| 01.1 | `01.1-home-portal/` | Understand the archive model and available routes. | Open Selected. |
| 01.2 | `01.2-selected/` | Choose a curated artifact without treating it as a trophy case. | Open an artifact. |
| 01.3 | `01.3-artifact-detail/` | Understand one artifact and its related context. | Scenario success. |

---

## Phase 4 Notes

Home / Portal should be compact and structural. It should not use a large hero claim or personal-brand pitch.

# 06: Potential Collaborator's Low-Pressure Contact

**Project:** Nathan Mike Sidi Bakari
**Created:** 2026-04-21
**Method:** Whiteport Design Studio (WDS)

---

## Transaction

**What this scenario covers:**
A collaborator sees enough overlap through entries and finds a restrained contact route.

---

## Business Goal

**Goal:** Keep opportunities emergent.
**Objective:** Contact is possible when there is genuine intersection, but it is not the site's organizing objective.

---

## User & Situation

**Visitor Mode:** Potential Collaborator (Secondary)
**Situation:** They have inspected relevant work and want to understand how to reach out without entering a sales flow.

---

## Mental State

**Trigger:** Related entries suggest a real overlap worth discussing.

**Hope:** Contact feels simple, respectful, and context-aware.

**Worry:** Outreach will feel transactional, over-designed, or pushed.

---

## Device & Starting Point

**Device:** Desktop or mobile.
**Entry:** They start from a relevant Entry Detail page after browsing related work.

---

## Best Outcome

**User Success:**
The collaborator finds a clear contact method and understands the kind of outreach that fits the site.

**Archive Success:**
The site permits contact without compromising its non-instrumental posture.

---

## Shortest Path

1. **Entry Detail** - Collaborator confirms relevant overlap.
2. **About** - Collaborator reads a minimal identity statement for context.
3. **Contact** - Collaborator finds a low-emphasis contact method. ✓

---

## Trigger Map Connections

**Visitor Mode:** Potential Collaborator (Secondary)

**Driving Forces Addressed:**

- ✅ **Want:** Low-pressure contact.
- ✅ **Want:** Cross-domain compatibility.
- ❌ **Fear:** Forced outreach.
- ❌ **Fear:** Unclear interests.

**Archive Goal:** Keep opportunities emergent.

---

## Scenario Steps

| Step | Folder | Purpose | Exit Action |
|------|--------|---------|-------------|
| 06.1 | `06.1-entry-detail/` | Confirm that contact has a real reason. | Open About. |
| 06.2 | `06.2-about/` | Get minimal identity context. | Open Contact. |
| 06.3 | `06.3-contact/` | Find a restrained contact route. | Scenario success. |

---

## Phase 4 Notes

Contact should be available from navigation and relevant contexts, but not styled as the primary objective of the site.

## What Belongs Here

The design system should capture reusable patterns that emerge from real page specifications and implementation.

For this project, the starting constraint is:

- Aesthetic: minimalist
- Tone: informal but precise
- Personality: human, exploratory, non-corporate
- Component library: Tailwind only

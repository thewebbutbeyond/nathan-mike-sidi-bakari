## Must Have MVP

- Structured artifact content model.
- Timeline.
- Collections and tags.
- Artifact detail pages.
- Home / Portal.
- Selected.
- Notes / Thoughts.
- Related artifact links.

---

## Consider for MVP

- RSS for Notes.
- About.
- Contact.

These are strategically useful but should remain restrained. RSS supports the editorial model; About and Contact support external visitors without changing the archive-first posture.

---

## Defer

- Search: defer until there is enough content to justify search behavior.
- CMS/admin workflow: defer until file-based publishing becomes a real burden.
- Analytics: defer unless there is a clear question worth measuring.

---

## Strategic Rationale

The highest-scoring features all serve the same core requirement: artifacts must be durable, contextual, and accessible through both time and identity.

The MVP should not begin with a sophisticated visual brand or conversion surface. It should begin with a content model and navigation structures that make the archive coherent.

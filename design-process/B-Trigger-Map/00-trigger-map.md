This project uses **simplified strategic analysis**. The trigger map should clarify why different visitors might explore without turning the site into a conversion funnel.

1. **Self / Future Nathan** - continuity, recall, and longitudinal trace
2. **Curious Observer** - non-linear exploration across domains
3. **Potential Collaborator** - finding authentic intersections of interest
4. **Professional Observer** - inferring competence from artifacts rather than claims
